		Python→Java: InputMismatchException, NumberFormatException 

		Java→Python: ValueError, EOFError

	C. Index/Key Access Issues

		Python→Java: ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException, StringIndexOutOfBoundsException, IndexOutOfBoundsException 

		Java→Python: IndexError, KeyError

	D. Missing State & Invalid Reference

		Python→Java: NullPointerException, NoSuchElementException, UnsupportedOperationException 

		Java→Python: AttributeError, NameError, UnboundLocalError, TypeError

	E. Arithmetic Errors

		Python→Java: ArithmeticException 

		Java→Python: ZeroDivisionError, OverflowError 

	F. Resource Exhaustion

		Python→Java: StackOverflowError, OutOfMemoryError 

		Java→Python: RecursionError
